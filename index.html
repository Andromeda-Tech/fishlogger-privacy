FishLogger 🎣

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

FishLogger ("the app") is a personal fishing logbook published by Andromeda Tech. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and your choices. We keep it simple: your logbook is yours.

What we collect

  • Account information. When you create an account we store your email address (and an encrypted password) so you can sign in. Authentication is handled by Supabase. If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your email and basic profile from that provider to create your account.
  • Your catches. Information you enter about a catch — species, location name, bait, conditions, notes, the date/time, and an optional photo.
  • Location. If you tap "use my location" or drop a pin, we store the coordinates you choose for that catch or hotspot. Location is only accessed when you ask for it; the app does not track you in the background.
  • Photos. Photos you attach to a catch are uploaded to private storage. The app accesses your camera or photo library only when you choose to add a photo.

We do not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, or contacts, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

How we use it

Your data is used solely to provide the app's features — saving and showing your catches, photos, and hotspots, and keeping you signed in. We do not sell your data or share it with third parties for advertising.

Where it's stored and who can see it

  • Data is stored on Supabase (Postgres database + file storage).
  • Row-level security ensures each angler can only read or modify their own catches, hotspots, and photos. Photos live in a private bucket and are served only through short-lived signed links you generate.
  • Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS).
  • Maps are provided by Google Maps / Apple Maps; weather (optional auto-fill) is provided by Open-Meteo using the coordinates you select.

Your choices and rights

  • Edit or delete any catch or hotspot from within the app at any time.
  • Delete your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below; we will remove your account, catches, hotspots, and photos.
  • You can revoke camera, photo, or location permission at any time in your device settings (some features won't work without them).

Children

FishLogger is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
